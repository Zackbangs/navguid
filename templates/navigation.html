📘 How NavGuid Works

▶
1. Speak or enter destination Start navigation by voice command or by entering the destination manually.
2. Generate route NavGuid prepares indoor or outdoor guidance based on your selected path.
3. Follow live instructions Live GPS tracking updates each step automatically as you move.
4. Stay aware of hazards Camera-based hazard monitoring gives extra safety support in real time.
